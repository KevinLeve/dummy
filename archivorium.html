Synopsis

Set across a single Continental winter, the film follows an archivist entrusted with restoring a damaged reel of unknown origin. As the frames are pieced back together, the boundary between the film being restored and the town restoring it begins, quietly, to blur. Shot largely by lantern and window light, it remains one of the most technically demanding restorations in the collection's history.
